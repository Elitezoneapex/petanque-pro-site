What you should know.

How does the free trial work?
Download the app free and use every feature for 7 days, no commitment. At the end of the trial, a single $3.99 purchase unlocks Pétanque Pro for life. No auto-renewing subscription, no surprises.
Is the purchase truly one-time?
Yes. For $3.99 you get Pétanque Pro forever, on every Apple device tied to the same Apple ID. Updates are free. Only Club Management ($9.99/month) and the optional Premium Court Pack are sold separately.
Does Pétanque Pro work offline?
Yes. AI play, the scorekeeper and your full game history work fully offline. A connection is only needed for online multiplayer and Club Management.
How does Club Management work?
The Club Management subscription ($9.99/month) lets you create a club, invite members with an invite code, run round-robin or single-elimination tournaments, and track each player's stats over time. Built for local clubs and pétanque associations.
Is my data private?
Pétanque Pro contains no advertising and no trackers. Your local games stay on your device. If you create an optional account for online multiplayer, only your display name is shared with opponents. Read the privacy policy.
